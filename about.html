About Me

Brand designer and digital specialist creating impactful identities for international agencies and Nepal's e-service sector

My Professional Journey

I specialize in creating comprehensive branding solutions for international service agencies, particularly those operating across multiple countries. My most significant project involved rebranding a multinational appointment booking agency, where I developed their complete visual identity - from core logo design to social media branding - creating a flexible system that maintains consistency across cultural contexts.

In parallel, I've worked extensively with Nepal's growing e-service sector, developing platforms that make essential services more accessible. For the appointment booking agency, I combined strategic branding with technical solutions to create a user-friendly digital experience that increased their user engagement by 40% across platforms while serving clients in 7 different countries.

What connects these projects is my ability to create visual systems that work locally while meeting international standards, whether for digital services or physical branding applications.
